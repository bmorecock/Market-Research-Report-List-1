Routine Maintenance Testing is performed regularly to assess and maintain the condition of electrical systems to ensure optimal performance and safety. Special Maintenance Testing is done when specific issues or concerns arise, such as troubleshooting faulty electrical equipment or testing after repairs or modifications.</p></p>
