<p><p>Terahertz imaging technology refers to the use of electromagnetic waves in the terahertz frequency range for various imaging applications. There are two main types of markets in this field. Active Terahertz Imaging Systems involve the generation of terahertz waves and their subsequent detection after interaction with objects, enabling active imaging capabilities. On the other hand, Passive Terahertz Imaging Systems rely on detecting naturally emitted or reflected terahertz waves from objects, providing passive imaging capabilities. Both markets cater to different needs and applications in fields such as security, medical imaging, and material characterization.</p></p>
