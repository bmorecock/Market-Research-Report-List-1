<p><strong>The Cell Culturing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Model System</li><li>Cell Integrated System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell culturing devices market can be categorized into two types: the cell model system and the cell integrated system. The cell model system refers to a device that mimics the environment of living cells for experimental purposes, allowing researchers to study and understand cell behavior. On the other hand, the cell integrated system involves a device that incorporates cells into a larger system or structure, such as a bioartificial organ or tissue, creating an environment that supports cell growth and functionality. These devices play a vital role in various research, pharmaceutical, and clinical applications.</p></p>
