<p><p>Dust control systems are used to suppress dust and improve air quality in various industries. Mobile controllers refer to compact and portable units that can be easily moved around. Handheld controllers are small devices designed for manual operation. Self-propelled controllers are equipped with wheels or tracks for autonomous movement. Tractor-mounted controllers are attached to tractors for efficient dust control in large areas. Trailed controllers are pulled by vehicles and provide dust suppression while being towed. Fixed controllers are stationary systems installed in specific locations for continuous dust control.</p></p>
