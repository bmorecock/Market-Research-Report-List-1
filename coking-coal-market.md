<p><p>The coking coal market types can be differentiated into four categories: hard coking coals (HCC), medium coking coal, semi-soft coking coal (SSCC), and pulverized coal injection (PCI) coal. Hard coking coals have high carbon content and are used in the production of coke for steelmaking. Medium coking coal has slightly lower carbon content and is also used in steelmaking. Semi-soft coking coal has even lower carbon content and is utilized in coke production. Lastly, pulverized coal injection coal is used as a supplemental fuel in blast furnaces during steelmaking processes.</p></p>
