<p><p>Stirling engines are divided into three market types based on their design and operational characteristics. Alpha Stirling engines have two power pistons and are typically used in low-power applications. Beta Stirling engines have one power piston and a displacer piston, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Gamma Stirling engines have only one piston and are primarily used in high-power applications. Each market type offers unique features and advantages, catering to different power requirements and usage scenarios in the Stirling engines market.</p></p>
