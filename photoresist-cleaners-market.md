<p><p>Photoresist cleaners are used in the semiconductor industry to remove photoresist materials from surfaces. Positive Photoresist (PR) strippers are designed to remove positive photoresist materials by breaking down the bonds between the photoresist and the substrate. On the other hand, Negative Photoresist (PR) strippers are used to dissolve negative photoresist materials, allowing for their easy removal. Both types of strippers play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and quality of semiconductor surfaces during the fabrication process.</p></p>
