These crystals offer excellent electrical and electromechanical properties. On the other hand, Type B crystals have a lower Curie temperature and find applications in low-temperature environments. They exhibit good piezoelectric properties and are commonly used in various electronic devices, such as ultrasound transducers and sensors.</p></p>
