Foil strain gauges consist of a thin metallic foil attached to a backing material, while wire strain gauges use a fine wire wound into a grid pattern. Semiconductor strain gauges utilize semiconductor materials to measure strain. These gauges are useful in applications where traditional strain gauges cannot withstand high temperatures, such as aerospace and automotive industries. They provide accurate and reliable measurements in extreme temperature conditions.</p></p>
