<p><p>Photovoltaic PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) Cells are a type of solar cell that are known for their higher efficiency and improved performance compared to traditional solar cells. These cells have gained popularity in the solar industry due to their higher conversion efficiency and lower production costs.</p><p>The Photovoltaic PERC Cells Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.
