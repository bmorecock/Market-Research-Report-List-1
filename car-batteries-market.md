<p><p>Car batteries are essential for the functioning of vehicles, and there are different types available in the market. VRLA batteries, also known as Valve Regulated Lead Acid batteries, are maintenance-free and suitable for start-stop systems. Flooded batteries, also known as wet cell batteries, require regular maintenance and are widely used in older vehicles. Other types include AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and Lithium-ion batteries. AGM batteries offer better performance and stability, while Lithium-ion batteries are known for their high energy density and long lifespan. Consumers can choose the battery type based on their vehicle's requirements and budget.</p></p>
