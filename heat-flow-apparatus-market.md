<p><p>The Heat Flow Apparatus market consists of two main types: the Shell Heat Transfer Apparatus and the Tube Heat Transfer Apparatus. The Shell Heat Transfer Apparatus is a device used to study the heat transfer process in a shell and tube heat exchanger. It allows researchers to analyze factors like temperature, flow rate, and heat transfer coefficient. On the other hand, the Tube Heat Transfer Apparatus specifically focuses on the heat transfer within the tubes of a heat exchanger. These apparatuses offer valuable insights into heat transfer phenomena for various industrial applications.</p></p>
