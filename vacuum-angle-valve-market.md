<p><p>The vacuum angle valve market comprises three types: Manual Angle Valve, Pneumatic Angle Valve, and Electromagnetic Angle Valve. Manual Angle Valves are operated manually to control the vacuum flow. Pneumatic Angle Valves use compressed air or gas to regulate the vacuum flow. Electromagnetic Angle Valves utilize an electric current to control the vacuum flow. Each valve type offers different advantages based on the application requirements, providing users with a variety of options to choose from when selecting the appropriate valve for their vacuum system.</p></p>
