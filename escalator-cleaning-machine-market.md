<p><p>The market for escalator cleaning machines can be broadly classified into two types: Walk Behind and Automatic. </p><p>Walk Behind machines are operated manually by a person who walks along the escalator, utilizing the machine to clean the steps and handrails. These machines require human effort but offer flexibility in terms of maneuverability and control.</p><p>Automatic machines, on the other hand, are designed to clean escalators without any human intervention. These machines are usually mounted onto the escalator and can operate independently, automatically detecting the steps and cleaning them efficiently. They offer convenience and save time for the operators.</p></p>
