<p><p>Combination starters are devices used to control and protect electric motors. There are two types of combination starters: non-reversing and reversing. Non-reversing combination starters are designed to start and stop motors in a single direction. They provide overload protection and can be used in applications where the motor operates in one direction only. On the other hand, reversing combination starters allow motors to operate in both forward and reverse directions. They include controls for reversing the motor's direction and provide necessary protection for each direction of operation.</p></p>
