<p><p>The portable sauna market consists of two main types: infrared saunas and steam saunas. Infrared saunas use infrared heaters to emit radiant heat that directly heats the body, offering a more gentle and even heat distribution. This type of sauna helps with detoxification, relaxation, and pain relief. On the other hand, steam saunas create hot, humid air by pouring water over heating elements. They provide a wet and intense heat that helps open pores, cleanse the skin, and promote respiratory wellness. Both types offer the convenience of portability, allowing users to enjoy sauna therapy wherever they go.</p></p>
