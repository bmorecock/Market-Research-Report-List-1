<p><p>Butter production lines come in two main market types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic butter production lines are highly mechanized and require minimal human intervention. They have advanced equipment and systems that automate most of the butter-making process, from churning the cream to packaging the butter. Semi-automatic butter production lines, on the other hand, involve a combination of manual and automated operations. While some tasks are performed by machines, there is still a significant need for human operators to monitor and control the production line.</p></p>
