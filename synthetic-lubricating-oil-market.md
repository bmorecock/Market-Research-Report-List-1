<p><p>Synthetic lubricating oil market consists of various types including organic esters, synthetic hydrocarbons, polyether, polysiloxane (silicone oil), containing oil, and phosphate market. Organic esters are derived from the reaction of organic acids and alcohols, offering excellent lubricity and thermal and oxidative stability. Synthetic hydrocarbons are made from synthesized hydrocarbon molecules, providing high resistance to oxidation and thermal stability. Polyether oils offer outstanding low-temperature fluidity and good resistance to oxidative degradation. Polysiloxane or silicone oils possess high thermal stability and resistance to oxidation. Containing oils combine synthetic and mineral oils for enhanced performance, while phosphate esters are fire-resistant and exhibit great anti-wear properties.</p></p>
