<p><p>The stretch wrapping machine market can be categorized into three types based on their automation level: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual machines. Fully automatic machines require minimal human intervention as they complete the entire wrapping process automatically. Semi-automatic machines need some manual assistance, such as attaching the film to the load, but the rest of the process is automated. Manual machines require complete manual intervention for the entire wrapping process, including attaching and cutting the film. The market offers these different types to cater to various packaging needs and customer preferences.</p></p>
