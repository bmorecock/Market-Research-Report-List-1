<p><p>The Cold Room market comprises two types: Single Chamber Cold Room and Multi Chamber Cold Room. Single Chamber Cold Rooms are designed to store products at a specific temperature range within a single large chamber. This type of cold room is suitable for businesses that require a general storage environment for various products. In contrast, Multi Chamber Cold Rooms consist of multiple chambers with different temperature ranges, allowing businesses to store different types of products separately at their required temperatures. This type of cold room is ideal for businesses with specific temperature requirements for different products.</p></p>
