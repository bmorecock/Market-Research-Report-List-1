<p><p>A Continuous Friction Tester (CFT) is a device used to measure the frictional properties of road surfaces. <p><p>The Continuous Friction Tester market comprises two main types: Portable Continuous Friction Tester (PCFT) and Benchtop Continuous Friction Tester (BCFT). PCFT is a portable device used to measure the friction properties of various surfaces, such as roads and airport runways. It can be easily transported and operated on-site, providing real-time data about friction levels. On the other hand, BCFT is a stationary machine that is typically used in labs or testing facilities. It offers controlled and standardized testing conditions to accurately measure the friction performance of different materials.</p></p>
