<p><p>Business planning software market types can be categorized into two main categories: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through an internet connection. It offers greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as users can access the software from anywhere. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and operated on a company's own servers, requiring dedicated hardware and IT staff. While it offers greater control and security, it may be more expensive and less flexible compared to cloud-based solutions.</p></p>
