<p><p>The Common Mode Inductor market consists of two major types: SMD Common Mode Inductor and Through Hole Common Mode Inductor. SMD Common Mode Inductor is a surface-mounted device widely used in compact electronic devices, providing effective noise suppression and filtering. On the other hand, Through Hole Common Mode Inductor is installed by inserting leads into holes on a circuit board, offering higher power handling capability and greater physical strength. Both types serve the purpose of reducing electromagnetic interference and improving the overall performance of electronic systems.</p></p>
