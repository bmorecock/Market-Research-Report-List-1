<p><p>The Steel Attic Ladder market can be categorized into two types: Adjustable and Non-Adjustable. Adjustable attic ladders are designed to have telescopic or folding mechanisms that allow the ladder's length to be adjusted to different ceiling heights. This makes them versatile and suitable for various attic spaces. On the other hand, Non-Adjustable attic ladders are fixed in length and cannot be modified. They are typically available in standard sizes and are suitable for attics with a consistent ceiling height. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the Steel Attic Ladder market.</p></p>
