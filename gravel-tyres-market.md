<p><p>Gravel tyres are specifically designed for off-road and gravel riding, providing enhanced traction and stability on loose and uneven surfaces. They are commonly used in gravel racing and adventure riding, as they can handle various terrains such as gravel roads, dirt paths, and even muddy conditions.</p><p>The gravel tyres market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, driven by the increasing popularity of gravel riding among cyclists. Factors such as the rising interest in adventure sports, the desire for exploring new terrains, and the growing number of gravel events and races have fueled the demand for these tyres. Additionally, advancements in tyre technology have led to the development of more durable and puncture-resistant gravel tyres, further driving their adoption.</p><p>The market analysis indicates that North America holds a significant share in the gravel tyres market, owing to the presence of a large number of gravel enthusiasts and a well-established cycling infrastructure. Manufacturers are focusing on producing wider tyres to provide better stability and comfort to riders. Moreover, tubeless setups are gaining popularity among gravel riders due to their lower rolling resistance and reduced risk of pinch flats.</p><p>Overall, the gravel tyres market is expected to continue its growth trajectory in the coming years.
