<p><p>The Pylon Market includes various types of towers used in power transmission systems. Tension Towers are designed to withstand the tension of conductors. Straight Towers provide a straight path for transmission lines. Corner Towers change the direction of lines. Transposition Towers facilitate the crossing of lines to prevent interference. Terminal Towers are used at the end of transmission lines. Spanning Towers support long spans between two towers. These different types of towers cater to specific requirements in power transmission, ensuring efficient and reliable electricity flow.</p></p>
