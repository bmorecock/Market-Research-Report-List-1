<p><p>Embedded fingerprint modules for physical access control are primarily categorized into two types: capacitive fingerprint modules and optical fingerprint modules. </p><p>Capacitive fingerprint modules use electrical current to create an image of the fingerprint pattern, while optical fingerprint modules use light to capture the fingerprint image. </p><p>Both types of modules offer secure and convenient access control solutions for various applications such as security systems, time and attendance systems, and door locks. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as cost, accuracy, and application requirements.</p></p>
