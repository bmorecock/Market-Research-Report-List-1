<p><p>Handheld vision screeners are devices used to test eyesight and diagnose vision problems. The market for handheld vision screeners can be categorized into two types: monocular measurement and binocular measurement. Monocular measurement involves testing one eye at a time, providing individual information about each eye's visual acuity. Binocular measurement, on the other hand, tests both eyes together, assessing how well they work together and provide a more comprehensive understanding of a patient's vision. These measurements aid healthcare professionals in identifying and addressing vision issues effectively.</p></p>
