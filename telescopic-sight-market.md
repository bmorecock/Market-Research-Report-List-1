<p><strong>The Telescopic Sight Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Collimating Optical Sight</li><li>Reflex Sight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telescopic sight market encompasses different types of sighting devices, including collimating optical sights and reflex sights. Collimating optical sights use a system of lenses to align and focus light, allowing for better target accuracy. They are often used in long-range shooting applications. On the other hand, reflex sights utilize a simple optical mechanism that reflects an illuminated reticle onto a lens, making it easier for shooters to acquire and track targets quickly. Reflex sights are commonly used in close-range and rapid shooting scenarios.</p></p>
