The market for PICCs can be categorized into two types: Power Injected PICCs and Conventional PICCs. Power Injected PICCs are designed to withstand higher pressure for delivering contrast agents during imaging tests. Conventional PICCs, on the other hand, are used for regular intravenous therapies. Both types of PICCs offer efficient and reliable access to the bloodstream, enabling effective treatment and reducing the need for repeated needle insertions.</p></p>
