<p><ul><li>Control of Push Button</li><li>Control of Motion Detector</li><li>Control of Other Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic door closer market consists of three main types: control of push button, control of motion detector, and control of other devices. The control of push button type is activated by a user pressing a button to either open or close the door. Control of motion detector type uses sensors to detect movement, automatically opening or closing the door in response. Control of other devices refers to integrating the door closer with external systems, such as access control or security systems, which determine when the door should open or close based on specific conditions.</p></p>
