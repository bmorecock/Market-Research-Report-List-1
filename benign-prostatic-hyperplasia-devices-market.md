<p><p>The benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) devices market includes various types of treatments for the condition. Transurethral resection of the prostate (TURP) involves removing excess prostate tissue through a resectoscope. Transurethral microwave therapy (TUMT) uses microwaves to heat and destroy prostate tissue. Transurethral needle ablation of the prostate (TUNA) involves the insertion of needles to deliver radiofrequency energy and destroy excess tissue. Laser surgery utilizes laser energy to vaporize or remove prostate tissue. Prostatic stenting involves the placement of a stent to keep the urethra open. Other treatments include prostatic artery embolization and water-induced thermotherapy, among others.</p></p>
