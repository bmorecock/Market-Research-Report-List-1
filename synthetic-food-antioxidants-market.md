The most commonly used synthetic food antioxidants include butylated hydroxyanisole (BHA), butylated hydroxytoluene (BHT), and tert-Butylhydroquinone (TBHQ). These antioxidants are widely used in various food products including oils and fats, bakery products, beverages, and dairy products.</p><p>The future outlook of the synthetic food antioxidants market is highly positive.
