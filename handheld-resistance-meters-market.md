<p><ul><li>Low Resistance Testing</li><li>Large Resistance Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld resistance meters are electronic devices used to measure the resistance of electrical circuits or components. The market for handheld resistance meters can be categorized into two main types: low resistance testing and large resistance testing. </p><p>Low resistance testing is focused on measuring small resistances in milliohms or microohms, typically used in applications where high precision is required, such as in electronics or automotive industries. On the other hand, large resistance testing involves measuring resistance values in megaohms or gigaohms, commonly used in industries like telecommunications or power distribution. Both types serve different purposes based on the specific resistance measurement requirements.</p></p>
