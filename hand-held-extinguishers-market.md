Foam extinguishers are suitable for putting out fires caused by flammable liquids and solid combustible materials. Carbon dioxide extinguishers, on the other hand, are specifically designed for fires involving electrical equipment and flammable liquids, as they do not leave any residue. Each type of extinguisher caters to different fire scenarios and can be an essential tool for fire safety.</p></p>
