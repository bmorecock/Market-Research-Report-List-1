<p><p>Multi-parameter patient monitoring equipment market can be categorized into high-acuity monitors, mid-acuity monitors, and low-acuity monitors based on the level of patient care they cater to. High-acuity monitors are designed for critical care settings where patients require continuous monitoring of multiple vital signs. Mid-acuity monitors are suitable for step-down units or general wards where patients need regular monitoring but not as intensive as in critical care. Low-acuity monitors are used for outpatient or home monitoring where patients require basic vital sign measurements periodically.</p></p>
