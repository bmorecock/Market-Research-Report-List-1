<p><p>Semiconductor stepper systems refer to the equipment used in the semiconductor industry to facilitate precise and controlled movements during the fabrication process. The market for semiconductor stepper systems can be divided into two main types: stepper motors system and drives system. Stepper motors system consists of motors that move in discrete steps, allowing for accurate positioning and control. On the other hand, drives system includes the electronic components that control the stepper motors, providing power and signals for their operation. Both types are crucial in ensuring efficient and accurate movements in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
