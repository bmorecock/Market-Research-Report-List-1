<p><strong>The Industrial Machine Vision System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PC-Based Vision System</li><li>Smart Camera-Based Vision System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial machine vision system market can be categorized into two types: PC-based vision systems and smart camera-based vision systems. PC-based vision systems utilize high-performance computers to process and analyze image data captured by cameras. They offer greater flexibility, advanced algorithms, and extensive customization options, making them suitable for complex applications. On the other hand, smart camera-based vision systems are compact, self-contained devices that integrate the camera, processing unit, and software. They are ideal for simple, standalone applications where cost, space, and simplicity are crucial factors.</p></p>
