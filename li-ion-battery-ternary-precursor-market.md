<p><p>The Li-ion battery ternary precursor market consists of two major types: NCM type and NCA type. NCM (Nickel Cobalt Manganese) type involves a combination of nickel, cobalt, and manganese, which provides a high energy density making it suitable for electric vehicles (EVs) and portable electronics. On the other hand, NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum) type incorporates nickel, cobalt, and aluminum, offering an even higher energy density, making it ideal for long-range EVs. These different types cater to various market segments, with NCM preferred for most EV applications and NCA for specific high-performance applications.</p></p>
