<p><strong>The Molecular Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Biosensors</li><li>Optical Biosensors</li><li>Thermal Biosensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molecular biosensors are analytical devices used to detect and measure the presence of specific molecules in biological samples. Electrochemical biosensors rely on the electrochemical interaction between the target molecule and the biosensor, while optical biosensors use light to measure the molecular interactions. Thermal biosensors work by monitoring the heat generated during molecular interactions, and piezoelectric biosensors detect molecular binding events through changes in mechanical vibrations. These biosensor types enable accurate and sensitive detection of various molecules, making them valuable tools in research, healthcare, and other industries.</p></p>
