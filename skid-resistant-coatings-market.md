<p><p>Skid-resistant coatings are used to provide traction and prevent slips and falls on various surfaces. There are two main types of skid-resistant coatings available in the market: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and easy to clean up with water. They are suitable for indoor applications. Solvent-based coatings are more durable and weather-resistant, making them ideal for outdoor use. They are also known for their quick drying time. Both coatings offer excellent slip resistance and help enhance safety in various settings.</p></p>
