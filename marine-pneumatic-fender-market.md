<p><p>Marine pneumatic fenders come in various types based on their construction and materials used. Rubber Mat-wire Net fenders have a rubber outer layer with a wire netting for added strength. Tire-Chain Net fenders are made from recycled tires and have a chain netting for durability. Fiber Net fenders use high-strength synthetic fibers for enhanced performance. Other types of fenders may include custom designs or combinations of different materials for specific applications in marine environments.</p></p>
