<p><p>Hand wipes are commonly available in two market types: canisters and packets. Canisters are larger containers that hold a higher quantity of hand wipes. They are designed for long-term use and often have a resealable lid to keep the wipes moist. Canisters are commonly found in places like offices, hospitals, and households. Packets, on the other hand, are smaller and more portable. They contain a limited number of wipes and are often individually packed for convenience. Packets are popular for on-the-go use and are frequently seen in travel kits, restaurants, and public places.</p></p>
