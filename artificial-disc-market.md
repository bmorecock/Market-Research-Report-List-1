<p><p>The Artificial Disc Market includes two major types: metal and biopolymer. Metal artificial discs are made of materials like titanium or cobalt chrome alloys, which offer durability and stability for spinal support. Biopolymer artificial discs, on the other hand, are made of bio-friendly materials like medical-grade polymers, which allow for more flexibility and natural movement of the spine. Both types aim to replace damaged spinal discs and alleviate related pain, but differ in materials used and mechanical characteristics.</p></p>
