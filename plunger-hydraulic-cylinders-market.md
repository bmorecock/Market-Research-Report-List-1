<p><p>Plunger hydraulic cylinders are categorized into two market types: single function and double function. Single function plunger hydraulic cylinders are designed to perform a specific task, such as lifting or pressing. They are used in applications where only one directional movement is required. On the other hand, double function plunger hydraulic cylinders offer the capability to perform two different tasks simultaneously. This makes them suitable for applications that require both extension and retraction movements or movements in multiple directions. These market types enable customers to choose the most appropriate hydraulic cylinder based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
