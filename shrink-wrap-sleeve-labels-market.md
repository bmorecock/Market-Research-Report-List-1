<p><p>Shrink wrap sleeve labels, also known as shrink sleeves, are a type of packaging labels that are applied to products using heat. They are made of plastic material, usually PVC or PETG, and have the ability to conform to the shape of the product when heated, providing 360-degree coverage. These labels offer various advantages such as durability, moisture resistance, and enhanced shelf appeal, making them popular in industries such as food and beverages, personal care, pharmaceuticals, and more.</p><p>The global shrink wrap sleeve labels market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is projected to continue growing at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period.
