<p><ul><li>Closed Circuit Dive Rebreathers</li><li>Semi-Closed Dive Rebreathers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dive rebreathers are advanced scuba diving equipment that recycle the diver's breath, allowing for longer dives and reduced gas consumption. There are two main types of dive rebreathers: closed circuit dive rebreathers and semi-closed dive rebreathers. Closed circuit dive rebreathers recycle all the exhaled gases, removing carbon dioxide and adding oxygen as needed. Semi-closed dive rebreathers release a portion of the exhaled gases back into the water while retaining some for reuse. Both types offer benefits such as extended dive times, reduced gas usage, and quieter operation.</p></p>
