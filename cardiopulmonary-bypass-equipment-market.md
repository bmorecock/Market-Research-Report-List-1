<p><p>Cardiopulmonary bypass equipment is a medical device used during cardiac surgeries to temporarily take over the function of the heart and lungs. It consists of a heart-lung machine, which maintains blood circulation and oxygenation while the heart is stopped for surgery. Cardiopulmonary bypass equipment ensures that the patient's blood is pumped and oxygenated, allowing the surgeon to operate on a still, bloodless heart.</p><p>The global cardiopulmonary bypass equipment market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue its upward trajectory. <p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass equipment market includes two types: single roller pump and double roller pump. Single roller pump cardiopulmonary bypass equipment uses a single roller to deliver blood flow during cardiac surgeries, while double roller pump cardiopulmonary bypass equipment involves the use of two rollers. These devices function by providing oxygenated blood to the patient, bypassing the heart and lungs temporarily. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining circulatory and respiratory functions during cardiac procedures.</p></p>
