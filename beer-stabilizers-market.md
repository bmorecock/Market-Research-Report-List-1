<p><p>Beer stabilizers are substances used in the brewing process to enhance the stability and clarity of the final product. PVPP/R-PVPP is a popular stabilizer that helps remove unwanted compounds and haze-causing proteins. Silica gel is used to absorb impurities and prevent undesirable flavors. Papain, a proteolytic enzyme, facilitates the breakdown of proteins and reduces chill haze. Other stabilizers such as bentonite and tannic acid are used to clarify beer and improve its shelf life. These stabilizers play a crucial role in maintaining the quality of beer.</p></p>
