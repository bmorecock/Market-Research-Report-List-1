<p><p>Spunbonded nonwoven fabrics are widely used in various applications, with some of the most common types being PP, PET, and PE. PP spunbonded nonwoven fabrics are made from polypropylene, offering high strength and excellent wicking properties. PET spunbonded nonwoven fabrics are made from polyester, providing high tensile strength and resistance to chemicals. PE spunbonded nonwoven fabrics are made from polyethylene, known for their flexibility and moisture resistance. Each type has its own unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
