<p><p>The Infectious Diseases Molecular Diagnostics market employs various types of technologies for accurate and rapid detection of infectious diseases. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a widely used technique that amplifies and analyzes specific DNA sequences for infectious agents. Molecular Hybridization involves the detection of complimentary DNA or RNA strands through their binding with fluorescent probes. Biochip technology allows for the simultaneous testing of multiple pathogens using a single device. Other diagnostics include next-generation sequencing, loop-mediated isothermal amplification, and nucleic acid sequence-based amplification, among others. These technologies play a crucial role in identifying and managing infectious diseases.</p></p>
