<p><p>Corporate car-sharing refers to the practice of sharing company-owned vehicles among employees, which can be categorized into two types: two-way and one-way markets. In a two-way market, employees pick up and return the vehicles to the same location, using the vehicle for a specific time period. On the other hand, in a one-way market, employees can pick up a vehicle at one location and return it to a different location, allowing for flexibility in the usage of the vehicles. Both types of car-sharing markets offer convenience and cost-saving benefits to corporations.</p></p>
