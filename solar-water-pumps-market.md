<p><p>The Solar Water Pumps market includes various types of pumps that run on solar energy. DC Surface Suction pumps are installed at the surface and use direct current to draw water from a source. AC Submersible pumps are submerged underwater and powered by alternating current. DC Submersible pumps are also submerged but utilize direct current. AC Surface Pumps are installed above ground and operate on alternating current. These different types of solar water pumps cater to specific requirements and environments, providing efficient water supply using renewable energy sources.</p></p>
