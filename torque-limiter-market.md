<p><ul><li>Friction Plate</li><li>Magnetic Particle</li><li>Magnetic Hysteresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Torque limiters are devices used to prevent damage from excessive torque in machinery and equipment. The three main types are friction plate, magnetic particle, and magnetic hysteresis. Friction plate torque limiters use friction to limit torque, while magnetic particle torque limiters use a magnetic field to control torque. Magnetic hysteresis torque limiters work by utilizing the magnetic properties of a material to regulate torque. Each type of torque limiter offers unique benefits and is used in various applications to ensure the safe and efficient operation of machinery.</p></p>
