<p><p>Single Use Valves are categorized into various types based on their design and functionality. 2/2-Way Valves are commonly used and control the flow of fluid by either opening or closing the passage. T Bodies Valves are designed in the shape of a T and allow fluid to pass through different arms of the valve. Angle Bodies Valves are bent at a right angle and regulate fluid flow through the valve. Other types include Diaphragm Valves, which use a flexible diaphragm to control flow, and Pinch Valves, which pinch a flexible tube to control the flow.</p></p>
