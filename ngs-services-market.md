<p><p>NGS Services market refers to the market for Next-Generation Sequencing services. RNA-Seq is a type of NGS service that analyzes the transcriptome, helping researchers understand which genes are active in a specific sample. This technology aids in identifying gene expression patterns and discovering novel RNA transcripts. Exome market, on the other hand, focuses on sequencing only the protein-coding regions of the genome. This targeted approach is used to identify genetic variations within specific genes that may be linked to diseases or other traits of interest. Both RNA-Seq and Exome sequencing play crucial roles in genomics research and personalized medicine.</p></p>
