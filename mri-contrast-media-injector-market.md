<p><p>The MRI Contrast Media Injector market includes two types of injectors: single-head and dual-head contrast injectors. A single-head contrast injector is equipped with one injector head, which allows for the injection of contrast media into patients during an MRI scan. On the other hand, a dual-head contrast injector consists of two injector heads, enabling simultaneous injection of contrast media to both arms of the patient. These injectors play a crucial role in enhancing the visibility and accuracy of MRI scans by providing a controlled and precise injection of contrast media.</p></p>
