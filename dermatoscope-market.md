<p><p>The dermatoscope market consists of two main types: traditional dermatoscopes and digital dermatoscopes. Traditional dermatoscopes are handheld devices that use a magnifying lens to examine the skin and detect potential skin conditions or abnormalities. They rely on the expertise of dermatologists to analyze the images produced. On the other hand, digital dermatoscopes capture high-resolution images of the skin and store them digitally. These images can be easily shared and analyzed remotely by dermatologists and other healthcare professionals, enabling quicker diagnosis and treatment decisions.</p></p>
