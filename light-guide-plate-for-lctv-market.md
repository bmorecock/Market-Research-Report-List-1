<p><p>Light Guide Plates (LGP) are core components in LCTV (Liquid Crystal Display Television) screens, responsible for evenly distributing backlighting. There are two main market types for LGPs: Print LGP and Print-less LGP. Print LGPs incorporate patterns directly printed on the surface, enabling light diffusion. Meanwhile, Print-less LGPs feature a microstructure on the surface without any printed patterns, ensuring more uniform light propagation. Both market types serve the purpose of enhancing the quality and uniformity of the backlighting in LCTVs.</p></p>
