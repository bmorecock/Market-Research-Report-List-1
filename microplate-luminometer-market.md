<p><ul><li>Singlemode Microplate Luminometer</li><li>Multimode Microplate Luminometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microplate luminometer market consists of two main types: single-mode and multimode microplate luminometers. Single-mode microplate luminometers are designed to perform a specific type of measurement, such as luminescence or fluorescence. They are ideal for users who focus on a particular application. On the other hand, multimode microplate luminometers offer multiple measurement modes, including luminescence, fluorescence, and absorbance, making them versatile for various applications. They are suitable for researchers and laboratories requiring flexibility and the ability to perform different types of assays on the same instrument.</p></p>
