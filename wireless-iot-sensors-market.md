<p><p>Wireless IoT sensors market can be classified into two types. Firstly, the traditional wireless technology market encompasses sensors that use Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Zigbee protocols for communication. These technologies are widely adopted due to their higher data transfer rates and shorter range. Secondly, the LPWANs (Low Power Wide Area Networks) technology market includes sensors that use low-power, long-range networks such as LoRaWAN, Sigfox, and NB-IoT. These technologies are preferred for their ability to transmit data over longer distances with low power consumption, making them suitable for applications in remote and low-power environments.</p></p>
