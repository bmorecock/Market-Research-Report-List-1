<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Railway Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1- Single Crossover</li><li>2- Double Crossover</li><li>3- Track Crossing</li><li>4- Slip Switch</li><li>5- Lapped Turnouts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>1. Single Crossover: A railway switch that allows trains to move from one track to another in a single direction.</p><p>2. Double Crossover: A railway switch that enables trains to move from one track to another in two different directions.</p><p>3. Track Crossing: A railway switch that facilitates trains to cross over another track, often used in locations where multiple tracks intersect.</p><p>4. Slip Switch: A railway switch that allows trains to switch between straight and curved tracks, providing flexibility in rail directions.</p><p>5. Lapped Turnouts: A railway switch that consists of multiple curved turnouts overlapping each other, enabling trains to switch between parallel tracks in a compact space.</p></p>
