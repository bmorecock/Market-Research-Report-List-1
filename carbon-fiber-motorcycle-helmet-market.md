<p><p>The carbon fiber motorcycle helmet market consists of various types, including Full Face Helmet, Open Face Helmet, and Half Helmet. A Full Face Helmet provides complete head and face protection, making it ideal for high-speed riding. An Open Face Helmet offers protection to the head but leaves the face exposed, suitable for those who prefer a more relaxed riding experience. A Half Helmet covers only the top and back of the head, leaving the face and lower part unprotected, often favored by cruiser or touring riders looking for a lightweight option.</p></p>
