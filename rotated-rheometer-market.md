<p><strong>The Rotated Rheometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stress Controlled Type Rotated Rheometer</li><li>Strain Controlled Type Rotated Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A stress-controlled type rotated rheometer is a device used to measure the response of a material to applied stress. It applies a set stress level to the material and measures the resulting strain. This type of rheometer is commonly used in industries such as polymers and chemicals to determine the flow behavior and viscosity of materials. </p><p>On the other hand, a strain-controlled type rotated rheometer applies a set strain level to the material and measures the resulting stress. This type of rheometer is also used in industries like food and pharmaceuticals to assess the deformability and mechanical properties of materials under strain. Both types of rotated rheometers play a crucial role in various industries by providing valuable data for product development and quality control purposes.</p></p>
