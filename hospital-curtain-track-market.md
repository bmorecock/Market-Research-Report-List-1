<p><strong>The Hospital Curtain Track Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>45 Degree Bend</li><li>90 Degree Bend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hospital Curtain Track Market comprises three types: straight, 45-degree bend, and 90-degree bend tracks. Straight tracks are utilized in hospital settings to create partitions or separate areas. They are perfect for dividing larger spaces into smaller sections. The 45-degree bend tracks are specifically designed to connect two straight tracks at a 45-degree angle, enabling the creation of L-shaped or T-shaped layouts. On the other hand, the 90-degree bend tracks allow for the merging of two straight tracks at a 90-degree angle, enabling partition creation in corners.</p></p>
