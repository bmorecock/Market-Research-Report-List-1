<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CMOS 3D Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Backside-illuminated(BSI)</li><li>BSI Stacked</li><li>Front-illuminated(FI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS 3D image sensor market consists of three types: backside-illuminated (BSI), BSI stacked, and front-illuminated (FI). BSI sensors are designed to capture more light by placing the photodiode behind all electronic components. BSI stacked sensors go a step further and vertically integrate image sensors and other components to improve performance. On the other hand, FI sensors have a traditional layout with electronic components in front of the photodiode. Each type offers different advantages in terms of light sensitivity, resolution, and overall image quality.</p></p>
