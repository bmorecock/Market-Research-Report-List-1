<p><p>Gravity casting machines are used to manufacture metal products by pouring molten metal into a mold under the force of gravity. Two types of gravity casting machines exist - tilting and fixed machines. Tilting gravity casting machines allow the mold to be tilted at an angle, facilitating the removal of castings and reducing material wastage. Fixed gravity casting machines, on the other hand, keep the mold stationary during the casting process. Both types play a crucial role in the metal casting industry, offering flexibility and efficiency in their respective operations.</p></p>
