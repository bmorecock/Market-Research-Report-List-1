<p><p>Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) is a type of dialysis that allows individuals with kidney failure to clean their blood at their own convenience without the need for a machine. CAPD bags are used to hold the dialysis solution, which flows in and out of the peritoneal cavity to remove waste products and excess fluid. The bag needs to be changed several times throughout the day.</p><p>The Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis Bag Market is witnessing significant growth due to the rising prevalence of chronic kidney diseases and the increasing number of patients opting for peritoneal dialysis as a treatment option. Additionally, the advantages of CAPD bags such as portability and convenience are also driving market growth.</p><p>The market is also benefiting from technological advancements in CAPD bags, as manufacturers are developing more user-friendly and efficient designs. For example, the introduction of bags with integrated connectors and improved seal systems has reduced the risk of contamination and improved ease of use. This has further boosted the adoption of CAPD bags.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a growing trend of using automated peritoneal dialysis (APD) systems, which are more efficient and require less manual intervention compared to CAPD.
