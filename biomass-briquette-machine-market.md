<p><p>The biomass briquette machine market consists of three main types: mechanical stamping briquette press, screw briquetting machine, and hydraulic briquette machine. The mechanical stamping briquette press is operated by a crankshaft and compresses biomass materials into briquettes. The screw briquetting machine uses a screw to compress and extrude biomass materials into briquettes. The hydraulic briquette machine, on the other hand, employs a hydraulic system to compress biomass materials into briquettes. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the biomass briquette industry.</p></p>
