<p><p>The motorized mobile X-ray system market comprises two types: digital radiography (DR) and analog radiography. Digital radiography involves capturing X-ray images using digital sensors, which are then converted into digital signals and displayed on a computer. It allows for quick image acquisition and manipulation, offering better resolution and image quality. Analog radiography, on the other hand, uses traditional X-ray film for image capture, which requires chemical processing. Although less advanced, it remains in use due to its lower cost and suitability for certain applications.</p></p>
