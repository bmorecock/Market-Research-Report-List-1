<p><p>Analog AC drive, also known as variable frequency drive (VFD) or inverter, is an electronic device used to control the speed and torque of an AC motor by varying the frequency and voltage of the power supply. This is achieved through the conversion of fixed frequency AC power input to variable frequency AC power output. Analog AC drives find applications across various industries including manufacturing, oil and gas, chemical, energy, and others.</p><p>The analog AC drive market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period.
