<p><p>Liquid Particle Counting Systems are used to measure and analyze the number and sizes of particles in liquids. Offline Type systems are portable and used for sampling liquid particles from a specific location and analyzing them later in a laboratory setting. In-line Remote Type systems are integrated into a continuous liquid flow system, allowing real-time analysis of particles as they pass through. These systems provide immediate data for process control and monitoring, ensuring quality and safety in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and water treatment.</p></p>
