<p><ul><li>High Frequency</li><li>Normal Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vibrostand market can be categorized into two types: high frequency and normal frequency. High frequency vibrostands are specifically designed to handle tasks that require rapid vibrations, such as compacting soil or removing air bubbles from concrete. They offer fast and efficient performance, making them suitable for demanding applications. On the other hand, normal frequency vibrostands are more versatile and can be used for a wide range of tasks, including general compaction or mixing applications. They strike a balance between power and versatility, making them suitable for various job requirements.</p></p>
