<p><p>The fingerprint biometrics machine market consists of two main types: single finger and multi-finger machines. Single finger machines are designed to capture and authenticate the fingerprint of one finger at a time. These machines are commonly used in small-scale applications like access control systems and time attendance management. On the other hand, multi-finger machines are capable of capturing and matching multiple fingerprints simultaneously. These machines are often utilized in high-security environments such as border control and law enforcement agencies, where accurate identification of individuals is crucial.</p></p>
