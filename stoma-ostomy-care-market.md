<p><p>The stoma and ostomy care market includes two main types: ostomy care bags and ostomy care accessories. They come in different types such as one-piece or two-piece systems and are essential for managing waste for patients with colostomy, ileostomy, or urostomy. Ostomy care accessories, on the other hand, include products like skin barrier rings, belts, adhesive removers, deodorants, and pouch covers, which provide added comfort, protection, and convenience for stoma patients.</p></p>
