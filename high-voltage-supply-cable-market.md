<p><p>The High Voltage Supply Cable market consists of two main types: Single Mode and Multi-Mode cables. Single Mode cables are designed for long-distance transmission with a single light beam, using a small core size. They provide higher bandwidth and are suitable for applications that require high-speed and long-distance communication. On the other hand, Multi-Mode cables have a larger core size and are used for short-distance transmission. They offer lower bandwidth but are more cost-effective for applications with shorter distance requirements. Both types cater to different transmission needs within the High Voltage Supply Cable market.</p></p>
