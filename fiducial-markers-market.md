<p><p>Fiducial markers are used in various fields such as medical imaging and robotics to provide reference points. Polymer-based markers are made from synthetic materials that are durable and can be easily visualized in images. Metal-based markers, on the other hand, are typically made from materials such as gold or tantalum that offer strong visibility and stability. Lastly, other types of fiducial markers include biocompatible markers like hydrogel-based markers or markers based on radiopaque materials. All these markers serve to accurately locate and track specific points during procedures, aiding in precise measurements and navigation.</p></p>
