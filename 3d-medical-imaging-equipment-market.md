<p><p>3D medical imaging equipment market includes various types like X-Ray, Ultrasound, MRI, CT Scan, and Hybrid Imaging. X-Ray machines use electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the inside of the body. Ultrasound uses high-frequency sound waves to create images. MRI uses magnetic field and radio waves to generate detailed images. CT Scan combines X-Ray images to create cross-sectional images. Hybrid Imaging combines different imaging modalities for more accurate diagnosis and treatment planning in medical imaging.</p></p>
