<p><strong>The Amorphous Cores Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toroidal Cores</li><li>Cut Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amorphous cores market consists of different types such as toroidal cores and cut cores. Toroidal cores are donut-shaped and widely used due to their compact size, low magnetic leakage, and high efficiency in reducing electromagnetic interference. Cut cores, on the other hand, are made by stacking amorphous alloy strips with insulating layers, providing flexibility in the design and allowing for easier customization. Both toroidal cores and cut cores play crucial roles in applications like transformers, inductors, and magnetic amplifiers, enhancing their efficiency and performance.</p></p>
