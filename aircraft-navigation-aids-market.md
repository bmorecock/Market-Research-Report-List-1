<p><strong>The Aircraft Navigation Aids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-directional Beacons</li><li>Instrument Landing Systems (ILS)</li><li>Distance Measuring Equipment (DME)</li><li>Doppler VHF Omnidirectional Range (DVOR)</li><li>Inertial Navigation System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft Navigation Aids market comprises various types of navigational equipment used by aircraft for safe and efficient navigation. Non-directional Beacons provide pilots with a fixed radio frequency reference point. Instrument Landing Systems aid in precise vertical and horizontal guidance during aircraft landing. Distance Measuring Equipment measures the distance between aircraft and ground stations. Doppler VHF Omnidirectional Range provides pilots with accurate bearing and distance information. Inertial Navigation Systems use accelerometers and gyroscopes to provide position data. Other types of navigation aids include automatic direction finders, global navigation satellite systems, and radar systems.</p></p>
