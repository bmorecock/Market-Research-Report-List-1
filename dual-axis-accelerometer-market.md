<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The dual-axis accelerometer market comprises two main types: piezoelectric accelerometers and piezoresistive accelerometers. Piezoelectric accelerometers measure acceleration by detecting the voltage produced from a piezoelectric crystal's mechanical stress. These devices are preferred for high-frequency and wide dynamic range applications. On the other hand, piezoresistive accelerometers measure acceleration using a change in resistance caused by mechanical stress applied to a strain gauge. These accelerometers exhibit high sensitivity and stability, making them suitable for critical and precise applications.</p></p>
