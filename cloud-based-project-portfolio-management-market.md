<p><p>Cloud-based Project Portfolio Management market encompasses three types of cloud deployment models - Private Cloud, Public Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Private Cloud refers to a cloud infrastructure exclusively dedicated to a single organization, providing enhanced data security and control. Public Cloud, on the other hand, is a shared environment managed by a third-party provider, accessible to multiple organizations, offering cost-effectiveness and scalability. Hybrid Cloud combines the benefits of both private and public clouds, allowing organizations to utilize a mix of on-premises, private, and public cloud resources based on their requirements.</p></p>
