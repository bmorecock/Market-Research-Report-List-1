<p><p>The Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer (OADM) market can be divided into two main types: Fixed Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer and Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer. </p><p>Fixed OADM refers to a system where the add-drop locations remain fixed and cannot be reconfigured. It offers a cost-effective solution for networks with a stable traffic pattern.</p><p>On the other hand, Reconfigurable OADM allows for flexible add-drop locations that can be changed as per network requirements. It provides enhanced scalability and agility for dynamic networks, accommodating varying traffic patterns and future network expansions.</p></p>
