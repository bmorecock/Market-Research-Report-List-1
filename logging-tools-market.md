<p><strong>The Logging Tools Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Hole Logging</li><li>Cased Hole Logging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Logging tools are essential for assessing oil and gas reservoirs. Open hole logging refers to the process of gathering data on formation properties while drilling is still ongoing. This tool provides crucial information about the wellbore and helps determine its productivity potential. On the other hand, cased hole logging involves evaluating the condition of the well after it has been completed and cased. This tool helps identify any issues or damages that may have occurred and aids in optimizing production efficiency. Both open hole and cased hole logging are vital for effective reservoir management.</p></p>
