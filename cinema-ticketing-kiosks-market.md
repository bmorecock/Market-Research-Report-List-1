<p><p>Cinema ticketing kiosks are available in two main market types: IR touch screen and capacitive touch screen. In the IR touch screen market, infrared sensors detect the touch of a user, allowing them to navigate through the kiosk's options by simply touching the screen. On the other hand, the capacitive touch screen market relies on a conductive layer that senses the touch of human skin or a capacitive stylus. This type of touch screen provides a more sensitive and accurate response, enhancing the user's experience when interacting with the kiosk.</p></p>
