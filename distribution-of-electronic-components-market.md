<p><p>The distribution of electronic components can be classified into two main market types: active components and passive components. Active components are electronic devices that can amplify or control the flow of electrical signals, such as transistors and integrated circuits. These components require power in order to function properly. On the other hand, passive components do not require power and their main purpose is to store, filter, or regulate electrical signals. Examples of passive components include resistors, capacitors, and inductors. Both active and passive components are essential in building electronic circuits and systems.</p></p>
