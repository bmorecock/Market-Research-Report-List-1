<p><p>Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) market is divided into three main types: General Purpose PVC Resin, High Polymerization Degree PVC Resin, and Crosslinked PVC Resin. General Purpose PVC Resin is commonly used for everyday products like pipes, bottles, and credit cards. High Polymerization Degree PVC Resin is designed for high-strength applications such as roofing material and industrial pipes. Crosslinked PVC Resin is specifically formulated for added durability and resistance to heat and chemicals, making it ideal for wire insulation and flooring.</p></p>
