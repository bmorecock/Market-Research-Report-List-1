<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The smart soil moisture sensor market consists of two main types: volumetric soil moisture sensors and soil water potential sensors. Volumetric soil moisture sensors measure the actual water content in the soil, providing an accurate measurement of the soil's ability to retain water. On the other hand, soil water potential sensors measure the tension or energy in the water held by the soil particles, indicating the soil's ability to supply water to plants. Both types of sensors are crucial in helping farmers and gardeners optimize water usage and improve crop yields.</p></p>
