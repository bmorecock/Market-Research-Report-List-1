<p><p>Natural Killer (NK) cell therapy is a type of immunotherapy in which Natural Killer cells are used to treat various diseases, such as cancer. NK cell therapy can be classified into two types: NK cell therapies and NK cell directed antibodies. NK cell therapies involve using NK cells derived from donors or patients themselves to target and kill cancer cells. On the other hand, NK cell directed antibodies are monoclonal antibodies that specifically bind to certain molecules on cancer cells to enhance the killing effect of NK cells. Both approaches show promise in the development of targeted cancer treatments.</p></p>
