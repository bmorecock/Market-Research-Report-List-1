<p><p>Fiberglass storage containers are categorized into different market types based on the type of glass used in their production. E-glass is a widely used type, known for its electrical insulation properties. C-glass, on the other hand, is popular for its chemical resistance capabilities. A-glass is primarily used in applications requiring high mechanical strength and thermal shock resistance. Lastly, the "Others" category includes storage containers made from alternative types of glass fibers. These market types allow customers to choose storage containers based on their specific requirements, such as electrical insulation, chemical resistance, mechanical strength, or thermal shock resistance.</p></p>
