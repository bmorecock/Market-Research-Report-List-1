<p><p>The market for lined concentric butterfly valves can be categorized into various types, including PFA lined, PTFE lined, FEP lined, and others. PFA lined valves are coated with perfluoroalkoxy polymer, providing excellent chemical resistance. PTFE lined valves feature a lining made of polytetrafluoroethylene, known for its low friction and high-temperature resistance. FEP lined valves have a lining of fluorinated ethylene propylene, offering good chemical resistance and electrical insulation properties. The "others" category may include valves with different lining materials, such as PVDF or ETFE, catering to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
