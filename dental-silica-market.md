<p><p>Dental silica refers to a type of silica-based material used in the dental industry. There are several types of dental silica available, including precipitated silica, fumed silica, colloidal silica, and others. Precipitated silica is created through a chemical process and is commonly used as a reinforcing agent in dental materials. Fumed silica is produced through a high-temperature flame vaporization process and is often used as a thickening agent. Colloidal silica consists of tiny particles suspended in a liquid and is utilized in dental polishing and filling materials. The "others" category includes various silica-based materials used in specific dental applications.</p></p>
