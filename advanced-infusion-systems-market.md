<p><p>Advanced infusion systems market includes various types of products such as infusion pumps, intravenous sets, IV cannulas, and needleless connectors. Infusion pumps are devices that deliver fluids or medications into a patient's bloodstream in controlled amounts. Intravenous sets are used to provide a pathway for the delivery of fluids or medications through a vein. IV cannulas are used to access veins for the administration of fluids or medication directly into the bloodstream. Needleless connectors are used to connect various components of an infusion system without the need for a needle, reducing the risk of infection.</p></p>
