<p><p>Boron meters refer to devices used for measuring the concentration of boron in various solutions, such as water. The market for boron meters can be categorized into two types: tank sensor boron meters and surface sensor boron meters. Tank sensor boron meters are designed to be submerged in tanks or containers to measure the boron content within the liquid. On the other hand, surface sensor boron meters are used to measure the boron concentration on the surface of liquids or solutions without the need for immersion.</p></p>
