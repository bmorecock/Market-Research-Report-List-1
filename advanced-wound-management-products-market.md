<p><ul><li>Advanced Wound Dressings</li><li>Therapy Devices</li><li>Active Wound Care Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced wound management products market consists of three main types: advanced wound dressings, therapy devices, and active wound care products. Advanced wound dressings are specialized materials designed to promote healing in chronic wounds by providing moisture balance and protection. Therapy devices include negative pressure wound therapy systems, which use suction to remove fluid and promote healing, and hyperbaric oxygen therapy devices, which deliver oxygen to the wound to enhance healing. Active wound care products are formulations containing enzymes, growth factors, or other substances that aid in wound healing.</p></p>
