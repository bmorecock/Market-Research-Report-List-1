<p><p>Active chilled beams are HVAC systems that use a combination of inductions and radiant heat transfer to heat or cool a space. These systems are installed in the ceiling and use the movement of air to transfer heat. They consist of a coil, a heat exchanger, and an induction nozzle. The coil is used to heat or cool the air, while the heat exchanger maintains the desired temperature. The induction nozzle introduces room air into the system, creating a flow of air that drives the heat transfer process.</p><p>The active chilled beams market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for energy-efficient HVAC systems in commercial and residential buildings is a key factor driving market growth.
