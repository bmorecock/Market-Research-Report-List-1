<p><p>Cryogenic cold traps are used in various industries to capture and remove impurities from gas streams. There are two main types of cryogenic cold traps: embedded cold traps and split cold traps. Embedded cold traps are integrated into the system, allowing for continuous trapping of impurities. Split cold traps, on the other hand, are separate units that can be easily removed and replaced. Both types of cold traps are essential for maintaining the purity of gases in applications such as semiconductor manufacturing and pharmaceutical production.</p></p>
