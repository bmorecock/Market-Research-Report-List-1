<p><p>The online testing software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based testing software operates on the internet, allowing users to access the software and conduct tests remotely without the need for installation or maintenance. On-premises testing software, on the other hand, requires installation on local servers or computers, and all testing activities are managed within the organization's infrastructure. Both types offer unique benefits and considerations, such as ease of use and accessibility for cloud-based software, and data security and customization options for on-premises software.</p></p>
