<p><strong>The Photoelectric Effect Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Pulse Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoelectric effect sensor market comprises two main types: analog and pulse photoelectric sensors. Analog photoelectric sensors function by measuring the amount of light that reaches a receiver, providing continuous output. They are widely used in applications where precise measurement or detection is required. On the other hand, pulse photoelectric sensors emit light pulses and measure the reflection time, allowing for more accurate distance measurements. These sensors are commonly found in industrial automation and robotics where precise object detection and positioning are crucial.</p></p>
