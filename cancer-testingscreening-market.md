<p><p>Cancer testing/screening involves various methods to detect and diagnose cancer at early stages for effective treatment. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) utilizes powerful magnets and radio waves to create detailed images of the body, assisting in identifying tumors. Human Papillomavirus (HPV) Testing helps detect HPV, a leading cause of cervical cancer. Colonoscopy involves visual examination of the colon to identify abnormalities or polyps that may develop into cancer. Other methods include blood tests, genetic tests, and mammography. These screening methods aid in early detection and prevention of cancer, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
