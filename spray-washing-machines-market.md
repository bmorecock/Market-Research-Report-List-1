<p><p>Spray washing machines are categorized into two main types: top loader machines and front loader machines. Top loader machines have a lid at the top of the machine where clothes are loaded, while front loader machines have a door at the front for loading clothes. Top loader machines are generally more traditional and easier to load, while front loader machines are known for their efficiency and water-saving capabilities. Both types are popular choices for households looking for effective and convenient laundry solutions.</p></p>
