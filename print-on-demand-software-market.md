<p><p>In the Print On Demand Software market, there are two main types: integrated and independent markets. Integrated markets refer to platforms that have their own built-in printing capabilities, allowing users to design, customize, and print products all within the same software. On the other hand, independent markets serve as marketplaces where users can upload their designs and connect with print providers to fulfill orders. These platforms provide a wider range of options for customers to choose from and usually offer more flexibility in terms of printing partners.</p></p>
