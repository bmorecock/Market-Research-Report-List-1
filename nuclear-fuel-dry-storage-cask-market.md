<p><p>The Nuclear Fuel Dry Storage Cask market includes two types of storage casks: horizontal dry cask and vertical dry cask. Horizontal dry casks are designed to store spent nuclear fuel horizontally. They are cylindrical in shape and are usually placed underground for better protection. Vertical dry casks, on the other hand, are tall and vertically oriented. They offer efficient storage solutions by stacking the fuel assemblies vertically, reducing the space required. Both types of casks are used in the nuclear industry to safely store and manage spent nuclear fuel.</p></p>
