<p><p>Ovulation tests, also known as ovulation predictor kits (OPKs), are widely used by women trying to conceive or track their menstrual cycles. These tests detect a hormone called luteinizing hormone (LH) in urine, which surges just before ovulation occurs. By identifying the LH surge, women can determine the most fertile period of their menstrual cycle.</p><p>The global ovulation test or ovulation predictor kit market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory. The market is driven by various factors, including increasing awareness of fertility tracking methods, rising infertility rates, and the growing preference for non-invasive and cost-effective ovulation detection techniques.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of more accurate and user-friendly ovulation tests, further fueling market growth. Digital ovulation tests have gained popularity due to their ease of use and clear digital readings. These tests provide higher accuracy and eliminate the subjectivity of interpreting result lines.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing trend of delayed pregnancies and the growing number of women opting for assisted reproductive technologies (ART) have contributed to the demand for ovulation tests. These tests help women identify their fertile window, increasing their chances of conceiving naturally or during ART procedures.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has impacted the ovulation test market positively, as more couples are spending time at home, leading to an increase in family planning activities.</p><p>In conclusion, the ovulation test or ovulation predictor kit market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.20% during the forecast period.
