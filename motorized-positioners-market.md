<p><p>The motorized positioners market consists of two main types: electric type and piezoelectric type. Electric type positioners utilize electric motors to adjust and control the position of objects with high precision. These positioners are commonly used in various industrial and scientific applications where accurate positioning is crucial. Piezoelectric type positioners, on the other hand, utilize the piezoelectric effect to control the movement of objects. This type of positioner offers sub-nanometer precision and is often used in fields like microscopy, semiconductor manufacturing, and nanotechnology research.</p></p>
