<p><p>Neuroprotective products market includes Cholinesterase inhibitors and NMDA receptor antagonists. Cholinesterase inhibitors are medications that increase levels of neurotransmitters in the brain, helping to improve memory and cognitive function in conditions like Alzheimer's disease. NMDA receptor antagonists, on the other hand, block the activity of a specific receptor in the brain, which can help protect against damage from conditions such as stroke or neurodegenerative diseases. Both types of products play a crucial role in promoting brain health and protecting against neurological disorders.</p></p>
