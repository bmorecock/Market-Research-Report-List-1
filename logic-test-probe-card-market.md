<p><p>The Logic Test Probe Card market consists of three main types: Vertical Needle/Tip, Thin Film MLO, and Membrane. Vertical Needle/Tip probe cards feature vertically arranged metal needles or tips for precise electrical contact with test points. Thin Film MLO (Multi-Layer Organic) cards use microfabrication techniques to create thin film circuits on an organic substrate. Membrane probe cards employ a flexible membrane with conductive bumps to make contact with the device under test. These different probe card types cater to various testing needs and offer varying levels of precision and flexibility.</p></p>
