<p><p>The belt filters market comprises two main types: horizontal belt filters and vertical belt filters. Horizontal belt filters are designed with a horizontal belt that moves continuously and carries the filter media. They are used for large-scale applications and are known for their high processing capacity. On the other hand, vertical belt filters have a vertical belt that moves up and down, allowing for effective drainage of filtrate. They are more suitable for compact spaces and smaller-scale operations. Both types offer effective filtration solutions in various industries.</p></p>
