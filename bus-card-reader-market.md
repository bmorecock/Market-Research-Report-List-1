<p><p>In the bus card reader market, there are different types of technologies available. One type is contact technology, which requires the card to physically make contact with the reader for the transaction to occur. Another type is contactless technology, which uses radio frequency identification (RFID) to enable transactions without physical contact between the card and the reader. NFC (Near Field Communication) technology is another variant of contactless technology that allows for secure and convenient transactions by simply tapping the card on the reader.</p></p>
