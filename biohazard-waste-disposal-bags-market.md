<p><p>The biohazard waste disposal bags market is categorized into four types: HDPE (high-density polyethylene), LDPE (low-density polyethylene), cellophane, and polypropylene. HDPE bags are known for their strength and durability, making them suitable for handling heavy or sharp biomedical waste. LDPE bags are flexible and tear-resistant, making them ideal for lighter biomedical waste. Cellophane bags are transparent and bio-based, providing visibility and eco-friendly disposal options. Polypropylene bags are resistant to chemicals and high temperatures, making them suitable for hazardous waste. Each bag type serves specific purposes in the safe disposal of biohazard waste.</p></p>
