<p><strong>The Vehicle Active Safety Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Domain Controller</li><li>Distributed Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Active Safety Controller market has two main types: Domain Controller and Distributed Controller. A Domain Controller integrates multiple active safety functions, such as braking, steering, and radar systems, into a single unit. It enables efficient communication and increases safety by coordinating these functions. On the other hand, a Distributed Controller disperses active safety functions across multiple units in the vehicle, allowing for greater flexibility and redundancy. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety by ensuring the effective coordination and execution of active safety features.</p></p>
