<p><p>The buzzer market consists of different types of buzzers, including Piezo Buzzers and Magnetic Buzzers. Piezo Buzzers utilize the piezoelectric effect, meaning they generate sound through the vibration of a piezoelectric material when an electric signal is applied. On the other hand, Magnetic Buzzers use a magnetic coil and diaphragm to produce sound. Both types of buzzers are commonly used in various applications such as alarms, electronic devices, and automotive systems, providing audible alerts and notifications.</p></p>
