<p><p>Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Market includes three main types - P-PRP (Pure-PRP), L-PRP (Leukocyte-PRP), and L-PRF (Leukocyte-Platelet Rich Fibrin). P-PRP refers to plasma highly concentrated with platelets, which are important for tissue repair and regeneration. L-PRP contains both platelets and leukocytes, which aid in reducing inflammation. L-PRF is a more advanced version, with a three-dimensional fibrin matrix that enhances healing and tissue regeneration. These different types of PRP have varying applications in the medical field, providing innovative solutions for various conditions and injuries.</p></p>
