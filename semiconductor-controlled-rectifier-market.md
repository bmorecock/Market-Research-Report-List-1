<p><p>The semiconductor controlled rectifier (SCR) market includes two main types: Unidirectional SCR modules and Bidirectional SCR modules. Unidirectional SCR modules allow current to flow only in one direction, making them suitable for applications that require rectification of AC power into DC power. On the other hand, Bidirectional SCR modules enable current to flow in both directions, making them ideal for applications that demand bidirectional control of power flow, such as in motor drives and power supplies. These market segments cater to different needs in terms of power rectification and control functionality.</p></p>
