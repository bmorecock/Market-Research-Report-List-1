<p><strong>The Dry Strand Pelletizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dry Strand Pelletizers market can be classified into two types: manual and automatic. In the manual market, operators have to manually feed the strands of material into the pelletizer. This type of pelletizer is typically used for small-scale productions or when flexibility in pellet size and composition is required. On the other hand, in the automatic market, the feeding, cutting, and pelletizing processes are fully automated, reducing the need for manual intervention. This type of pelletizer is often used in large-scale productions where efficiency and high output are priorities.</p></p>
