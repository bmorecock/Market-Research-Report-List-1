<p><p>The Solar Vehicle market consists of two main types: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs). BEVs are fully electric vehicles powered solely by rechargeable batteries, storing energy from the sun. They produce zero emissions and offer a sustainable alternative to traditional gasoline-powered cars. On the other hand, HEVs combine both an internal combustion engine and an electric motor. They use solar energy to charge the battery and assist the engine, resulting in lower fuel consumption and reduced emissions compared to conventional vehicles.</p></p>
