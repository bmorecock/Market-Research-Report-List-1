<p><p>The Autonomous Mobile Robot (AMR) chassis market consists of three main types: two-wheeled chassis, four-wheeled chassis, and tracked chassis. Two-wheeled chassis offer increased agility and maneuverability, making them suitable for indoor environments and tight spaces. Four-wheeled chassis provide better stability and can carry heavier loads, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Tracked chassis offer superior traction on uneven terrain and can navigate obstacles effectively. Each type has its advantages, and the choice depends on the specific application requirements of the autonomous mobile robot.</p></p>
