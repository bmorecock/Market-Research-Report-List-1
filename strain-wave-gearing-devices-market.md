There are three main types of strain wave gearing devices: cup style, hat style, and pancake style. Cup style devices have a flexible cup-shaped outer gear and a rigid inner gear that meshes with it. Hat style devices have a rigid circular outer gear with a flexible inner gear that meshes with it. Pancake style devices have a flat shape, with a flexible gear sandwiched between two rigid gears. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
