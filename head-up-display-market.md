<p><strong>The Head-Up-Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Projector</li><li>Combiner</li><li>Video Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Head-Up Display (HUD) technology is categorized into three main types based on their display mechanism - Projector, Combiner, and Video Generator. Projector HUDs use a projector to display information onto a transparent screen in the driver's line of sight. Combiner HUDs combine reflected images with external light sources to display information. Video Generator HUDs use a video source to generate images directly onto a transparent screen. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
