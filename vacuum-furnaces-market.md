<p><strong>The Vacuum Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum quench furnaces</li><li>Vacuum brazing furnaces</li><li>Vacuum carburizing furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum furnaces are categorized into three main types based on their functions and applications: vacuum quench furnaces, vacuum brazing furnaces, and vacuum carburizing furnaces. Vacuum quench furnaces are used for rapid cooling of materials to achieve specific properties, while vacuum brazing furnaces are designed for joining materials using a brazing process under vacuum conditions. Vacuum carburizing furnaces are used for carburizing or surface hardening of materials in a low-pressure environment, resulting in improved wear resistance and durability.</p></p>
