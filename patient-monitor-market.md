<p><p>The patient monitor market can be categorized into three types - high-acuity monitors, mid-acuity monitors, and low-acuity monitors. High-acuity monitors are advanced systems used in intensive care units to monitor critical patients with complex medical conditions. Mid-acuity monitors are typically used in general care units to monitor patients with moderate medical needs. Low-acuity monitors are simple and portable devices used in non-intensive care settings like ambulatory surgery centers or home care, to monitor patients with stable conditions. These different types of monitors cater to the varying needs of healthcare facilities based on patient acuity levels.</p></p>
