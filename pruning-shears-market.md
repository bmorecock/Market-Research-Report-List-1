<p><p>The pruning shears market is categorized into three types: pneumatic pruning shears, electric pruning shears, and manual pruning shears. Pneumatic pruning shears are powered by compressed air and offer high cutting power suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Electric pruning shears run on electricity, providing ease of use and efficient cutting performance. Manual pruning shears require manual force and are suitable for light pruning tasks. These different types cater to varying user needs, offering versatility and options in the pruning shears market.</p></p>
