<p><p>The dispenser market is composed of two main types: desktop and floor type. Desktop dispensers are compact and designed for use on countertops or other small surfaces. They are ideal for dispensing small quantities of products such as hand sanitizer or soap. These dispensers are designed to hold and distribute larger volumes of products to accommodate a larger number of people.</p></p>
