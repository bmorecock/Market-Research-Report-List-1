<p><p>Diffusion furnace is a type of equipment used for the fabrication of semiconductors. Horizontal Tube Reactor and Vertical Tube Reactor are two common market types for diffusion furnaces. </p><p>In a Horizontal Tube Reactor, the tubes are positioned horizontally, allowing for a larger number of wafers to be processed simultaneously. This type is well-suited for high-volume production.</p><p>On the other hand, a Vertical Tube Reactor has the tubes arranged in a vertical orientation. This design provides better temperature uniformity and is often preferred for applications that require more precise control and smaller batch sizes.</p></p>
