<p><p>A sensor faucet, also known as an electronic faucet or touchless faucet, is a type of faucet that operates automatically by sensing the presence of a user's hands. This type of faucet is widely used in public restrooms and commercial settings due to its hygienic and efficient nature. On the other hand, touch button faucets require a physical touch to activate the water flow. This type of faucet is commonly found in residential settings and allows users to easily control the water flow and temperature.</p></p>
