<p><p>Titanium Dioxide Matting Agents are used to reduce the gloss of a surface. They are available in two main types: Powder and Gelatum. Powder matting agents are finely ground particles that are typically added during the manufacturing process to matte the surface. Gelatum matting agents are pre-dispersed in a liquid medium, making them easier to incorporate into formulations. Both types offer different advantages and are commonly used in industries such as coatings, plastics, and textiles to achieve desired levels of gloss and finish.</p></p>
