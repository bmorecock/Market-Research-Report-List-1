<p><p>The multi-color injection molding machine market is divided into two types: horizontal type and vertical type. A horizontal type machine has a horizontal layout, with the mold opening and closing on a horizontal axis. This is commonly used for producing large and complex products. On the other hand, a vertical type machine has a vertical layout, with the mold opening and closing on a vertical axis. This type of machine is suitable for producing small and medium-sized products, and it is known for its compact design and high precision.</p></p>
