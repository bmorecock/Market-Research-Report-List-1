<p><p>The disposable protective mask market is categorized into three types: medical masks, dust masks, and others. Dust masks, on the other hand, are primarily used in industrial settings to shield against dust, pollen, and other non-toxic particles. The "others" category includes masks used for personal protection against pollutants, allergies, and general-purpose non-medical uses. These masks are designed to be used once and then discarded, ensuring continuous safety and hygiene.</p></p>
