<p><p>The amorphous polyethylene terephthalate market includes various types such as chain extenders, nucleating agents, stabilizers, and impact modifiers. Chain extenders are added to increase the length of polymer chains, improving strength and flexibility. Nucleating agents enhance crystallization and improve mechanical properties. Stabilizers prevent degradation of the polymer due to heat or UV exposure. Impact modifiers increase the toughness and impact resistance of the material. These different types cater to specific requirements and applications in the market.</p></p>
