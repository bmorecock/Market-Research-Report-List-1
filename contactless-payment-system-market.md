<p><p>The contactless payment system market encompasses three main types: contactless cards, wearable devices, and contactless mobile payment. Contactless cards use NFC (Near Field Communication) or RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) technology to enable quick and secure payments by tapping the card on a compatible reader. Wearable devices, such as smartwatches or fitness trackers, feature built-in NFC technology that allows users to make payments with just a tap. Contactless mobile payment refers to the use of smartphones or other mobile devices with NFC capabilities to make secure payments by simply holding the device near a payment terminal.</p></p>
