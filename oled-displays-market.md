<p><p>The OLED displays market consists of various types, including Transparent OLEDs, Stacked OLEDs, and Inverted OLEDs. Transparent OLEDs have the ability to display information while still allowing transparency and are commonly used in applications like head-up displays. Stacked OLEDs involve integrating multiple OLED layers, enabling higher resolution and brightness. They are employed in products such as smartphones and televisions. Inverted OLEDs have a different structure where the cathode and anode layers are reversed. This design leads to improved brightness and efficiency, making them suitable for large-scale displays and lighting devices.</p></p>
