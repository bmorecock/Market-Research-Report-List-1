<p><p>A hysteroscopy operative sheath is a minimally invasive medical device used during hysteroscopy procedures. Hysteroscopy is a diagnostic or surgical procedure where a thin, lighted tube with a camera called a hysteroscope is inserted into the uterus through the vagina to examine and treat various conditions such as abnormal bleeding, polyps, fibroids, or adhesions.</p><p>The hysteroscopy operative sheath is designed to create a channel for the hysteroscope to pass through, enabling the surgeon to visualize the uterine cavity and perform necessary surgical procedures. It helps in maintaining the visualization, providing access for instrument passage, and irrigating the uterine cavity during the procedure.</p><p>The hysteroscopy operative sheath market is experiencing significant growth due to various factors such as the increasing prevalence of gynecological disorders, rising awareness about minimally invasive procedures, technological advancements in hysteroscopy devices, and the growing geriatric population.
