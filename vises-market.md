<p><p>Vises are tools used to hold objects in place during various operations. There are four main market types based on their operation: mechanical, hydraulic, pneumatic, and manual. Mechanical vises are operated manually using a handle or crank. Hydraulic vises use fluid pressure to grip objects tightly. Pneumatic vises use compressed air to secure objects.
