These cards are crucial in maintaining the quality and reliability of ICs, reducing production costs, and minimizing time-to-market.</p><p>The future outlook for the advanced wafer probe cards market is highly positive. <p><p>The advanced wafer probe card market includes three main types: needle type, vertical type, and MEMS type. Needle type probe cards consist of multiple thin needles that make contact with the wafer during testing. Vertical type probe cards use vertical probes to make contact with the wafer, allowing for larger probe pitch and higher integration. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) type probe cards utilize micro-scale mechanical structures to achieve accurate and reliable contact with the wafer. These different types cater to varying testing requirements and offer unique advantages in terms of precision, scalability, and reliability.</p></p>
