<p><p>The Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR) camera market can be classified into two types: cooled and uncooled cameras. Cooled MWIR cameras utilize a cooling mechanism to lower the temperature of the camera's detector, allowing for higher sensitivity and better image quality. These cameras are typically used in applications requiring long-range detection, such as military and surveillance. Uncooled MWIR cameras, on the other hand, do not require cooling and are generally smaller and more cost-effective. They find applications in various fields, including industrial inspections, thermography, and automotive safety systems.</p></p>
