<p><p>In the hospitality market, there are various types of lighting options available. Compact Fluorescent Lamps (CFLs) are energy-efficient and have a longer lifespan than traditional incandescent bulbs. Linear Fluorescent Lamps (LFLs) are commonly used for task lighting and can provide a bright, even light. High-Intensity Discharge (HID) lighting is often used for outdoor and large indoor areas, producing a high-intensity light. Light-Emitting Diode (LED) lighting is energy-efficient, has a long lifespan, and provides flexibility in color and intensity, making it suitable for various hospitality settings.</p></p>
