<p><strong>The Integrally Geared Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric</li><li>Velocity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrally geared compressors are classified into two market types based on their performance characteristics: volumetric and velocity market. </p><p>In the volumetric market, compressors are designed to deliver a fixed volume of gas per unit of time. They are suitable for applications where a constant flow rate is required, such as in chemical plants or natural gas processing.</p><p>On the other hand, compressors in the velocity market are designed to provide a constant pressure ratio. They are commonly used in applications where maintaining a steady pressure level is crucial, such as in air separation units or petroleum refineries.</p><p>These market types help classify integrally geared compressors based on their intended function and performance parameters.</p></p>
