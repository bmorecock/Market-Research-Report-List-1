<p><p>There are two main types of Medical Education Market: On-campus and Distance learning. On-campus medical education refers to attending classes physically at a medical school or university campus. It involves regular face-to-face interactions with professors and peers, providing hands-on experience through laboratory sessions and clinical rotations. On the other hand, distance learning enables students to pursue medical education remotely, usually through online platforms. It involves virtual classes, video lectures, and interactive discussions, allowing flexibility in terms of time and location, while still providing comprehensive medical education.</p></p>
