<p><p>The Cytomegalovirus (CMV) Assay market encompasses different types of diagnostic techniques used to detect CMV infections. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) involve detecting CMV-specific antibodies in a patient's blood sample. PCR Assays utilize the polymerase chain reaction technique to amplify and detect CMV DNA in samples. DNA Assays specifically target and identify CMV genetic material. Other types of CMV Assays could include hybrid capture assays or flow cytometry-based assays that detect CMV proteins or analyze the body's immune response. These techniques play a crucial role in diagnosing CMV infections and guiding appropriate treatment.</p></p>
