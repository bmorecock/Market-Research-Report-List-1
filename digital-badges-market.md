<p><p>The Digital Badges market can be classified into two types - Virtual Badges and Real Badges. Virtual Badges are digital representations awarded to individuals for achievements or skills gained in online platforms or virtual environments. They can be used to showcase expertise and accomplishments in a specific field. Real Badges, on the other hand, are physical badges that are awarded in offline settings, such as conferences, workshops, or events. These badges serve as tangible proof of an individual's achievements and can be displayed in person or on a physical resume or portfolio.</p></p>
