<p><p>Cogged belts, also known as v-belts, are power transmission components that are widely used in various industries for rotating machinery and equipment. They are commonly made of rubber or synthetic materials and are designed to handle high-speed and high-torque applications.</p><p>The cogged belts market has been experiencing steady growth over the years, and this trend is expected to continue in the foreseeable future. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for cogged belts in industries such as automotive, manufacturing, agriculture, and construction due to their superior performance and durability. One such trend is the increasing adoption of carbon fiber reinforced cogged belts, which offer higher strength, reduced weight, and improved flexibility.
