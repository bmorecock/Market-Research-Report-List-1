<p><p>The ECG disposable medical electrodes market encompasses various types, including needle electrodes, patch electrodes, and others. Needle electrodes are thin, metallic needles that are inserted directly into the skin to measure electrical signals. Patch electrodes, on the other hand, are adhesive patches attached to the skin and used to record ECG signals. The "others" category refers to alternative types of disposable electrodes, such as wet gel electrodes or dry electrodes, which are used for ECG monitoring. All these types serve the purpose of capturing and transmitting electrical signals for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
