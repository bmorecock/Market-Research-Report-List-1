<p><p>The Prescription Dermatological Medications market consists of two main types: oral medications and topical medications. Oral medications are ingested and absorbed systemically, working from the inside out to treat various skin conditions. They are commonly used for severe or widespread skin problems such as acne, psoriasis, and eczema. On the other hand, topical medications are applied directly to the skin surface, providing localized treatment for conditions like rashes, infections, and inflammation. These medications come in various forms such as creams, ointments, gels, and lotions, delivering targeted therapy directly to the affected area.</p></p>
