<p><p>Golf shoes can be divided into two main types: spiked and spikeless. Spiked golf shoes have metal or plastic spikes on the soles, providing superior traction on the golf course, especially on wet or hilly terrain. On the other hand, spikeless golf shoes have a flat, rubber sole which offers versatility and can be worn both on and off the course. While spiked shoes are preferred for their stability and grip, spikeless shoes are known for their comfort and convenience, making them suitable for casual golfers or those seeking multi-purpose footwear.</p></p>
