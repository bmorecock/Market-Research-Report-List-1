<p><p>Electrical Submersible Pump (ESP) is available in two main types: Openwell and Borewell markets. Openwell ESPs are used in shallow water sources where the pump is submerged in an open well or tank, suitable for applications such as irrigation and drainage. Borewell ESPs are designed for deeper water sources like borewells, delivering water to the surface for various purposes such as residential and industrial usage. Both types of ESPs offer efficient water pumping solutions for different depths and applications.</p></p>
