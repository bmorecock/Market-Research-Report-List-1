<p><p>Cardiovascular monitoring and diagnostic devices have various types that aid in detecting and monitoring heart-related conditions. ECG systems, also known as electrocardiogram systems, record and analyze the electrical activity of the heart. Holter monitors are portable devices that record and monitor the heart's activity continuously for a specific duration. Event monitors are similar but are triggered by the patient when symptoms occur. Implantable loop recorders are small devices placed under the skin to provide long-term monitoring. Other types may include wearable devices and mobile health applications. All these devices help in diagnosing and managing cardiovascular diseases effectively.</p></p>
