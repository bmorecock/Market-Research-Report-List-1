<p><p>The wind power transmission equipment market comprises various types of equipment such as gearboxes, couplings, and others. Couplings join different components within wind turbines, ensuring smooth transmission of power. The "others" category covers additional equipment like generators, shafts, and bearings, which play important roles in transmitting power from wind turbines to the grid. These transmission equipment types are vital for efficient wind power generation and distribution.</p></p>
