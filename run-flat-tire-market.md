<p><strong>The Run-flat Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Supporting</li><li>Support Ring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The run-flat tire market consists of two main types: self-supporting tires and support ring system tires. Self-supporting tires have reinforced sidewalls that can bear the weight of the vehicle, allowing the driver to continue driving even after experiencing a tire puncture or loss of pressure. On the other hand, support ring system tires utilize an additional support ring that is fitted onto the wheel, enabling the tire to maintain its shape and support the vehicle even in the event of a puncture. Both types provide drivers with the convenience of extended mobility in case of tire failure.</p></p>
