<p><p>A magnetic cell sorter is a laboratory instrument used for the separation and sorting of cells based on their magnetic properties. It utilizes magnetic beads, which are conjugated with antibodies or other ligands that specifically bind to target cells of interest. The sorter applies a magnetic field to the cell suspension, causing the labeled cells to be attracted towards the magnet and separate from the unlabeled cells.</p><p>The magnetic cell sorter market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
