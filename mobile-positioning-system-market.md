<p><p>Mobile Positioning System (MPS) market comprises several types of technologies, including Time of Arrival (TOA), Enhanced Observed Time Difference (E-OTD), and Assisted Global Positioning System (A-GPS). TOA determines the position of a mobile device by measuring the time it takes for signals to travel between the device and multiple base stations. E-OTD calculates location based on differences in received signal strength from multiple base stations. A-GPS utilizes both satellite and cellular networks to provide precise positioning data. These technologies enable accurate and reliable mobile device location tracking for various applications and services.</p></p>
