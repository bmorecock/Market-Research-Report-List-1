<p><strong>The Side Scan Sonars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-beam</li><li>Multi-beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Side scan sonars are used for underwater mapping and surveying. Single-beam sonars emit a single narrow beam of sound waves, providing a limited view of the seabed below. They are ideal for shallow water and small-scale surveys. On the other hand, multi-beam sonars emit multiple beams simultaneously, offering a wider coverage area and higher resolution images. They are suitable for deep water and large-scale surveys. Both types of sonars have their own unique advantages and are used in various underwater exploration applications.</p></p>
