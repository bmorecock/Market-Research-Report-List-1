<p><p>The Air Hydraulic Pump market comprises two main types: Single Acting Air Hydraulic Pump and Double Acting Air Hydraulic Pump. The Single Acting Pump utilizes air pressure to generate force in one direction only, making it suitable for applications such as lifting loads or pressurizing liquids. On the other hand, the Double Acting Pump generates force in both directions, enabling tasks such as pressing and clamping. These pumps find applications in various industries where hydraulic power is required for efficient and controlled operations.</p></p>
