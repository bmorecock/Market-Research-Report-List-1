<p><p>The water analytical instruments market consists of various types of instruments used to analyze water quality. Turbidometers measure the cloudiness or turbidity of water caused by suspended particles. Floc testers determine the effectiveness of coagulants and flocculants in water treatment. BOD (Biochemical Oxygen Demand) systems measure the level of organic compounds in water to assess its pollution level. Colorimeters and spectrophotometers quantify the color or concentration of specific substances in water. Electrochemistry instruments analyze water using electrochemical reactions. Chromatography instruments separate and identify different components of water samples based on their chemical properties.</p></p>
