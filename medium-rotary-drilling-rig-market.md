<p><p>The Medium Rotary Drilling Rig market is divided into two types: Crawler and Rubber-Tired. Crawler rigs are equipped with tracks to maneuver on rough terrains, providing stability and mobility for drilling operations. These rigs are commonly used in challenging locations such as rocky terrains or uneven surfaces. On the other hand, Rubber-Tired rigs are mounted on tires, offering enhanced mobility on relatively smoother surfaces. These rigs are often utilized in urban environments or areas with less demanding terrain. Both types cater to different drilling needs based on the terrain requirements.</p></p>
