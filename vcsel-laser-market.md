<p><p>VCSEL Laser refers to Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Laser, which finds applications in various industries. The VCSEL Laser market consists of two main types, namely single-mode VCSEL and multimode VCSEL. Single-mode VCSELs are designed for long-distance, high-speed data transmission with low power consumption, making them suitable for applications like data centers and telecommunications. On the other hand, multimode VCSELs are more suitable for short-range applications such as fiber optic sensors and industrial heating, offering lower cost and higher power output compared to single-mode VCSELs.
