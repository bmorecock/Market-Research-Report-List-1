<p><strong>The Safety Hypodermic Needles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Safety Needles</li><li>Passive Safety Needles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Safety hypodermic needles market can be categorized into two main types: active safety needles and passive safety needles. Active safety needles have built-in safety mechanisms that are activated before or after the injection, reducing the risk of needlestick injuries. Examples include needle retraction systems and shielding mechanisms. Passive safety needles, on the other hand, have safety features that are automatically activated during the injection process. They rely on the user's habit of activating and maintaining the safety measure. Both types aim to enhance healthcare worker safety and minimize the transmission of bloodborne diseases.</p></p>
