Manufacturers are also focusing on launching products that cater to specific dietary needs, such as gluten-free, low-carb, or vegan options.</p><p>Overall, the RTD beverages market is expected to flourish in the coming years, driven by factors such as convenience, health consciousness, and product innovation.</p></p>
