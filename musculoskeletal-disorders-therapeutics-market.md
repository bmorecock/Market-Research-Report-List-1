<p><p>The Musculoskeletal Disorders Therapeutics Market consists of two main types of treatments: Biologics and Small Molecules. They can include monoclonal antibodies, growth factors, or cell therapies. Small molecules, on the other hand, are synthetic compounds that typically target enzymes or receptors to modulate biochemical processes in the body. Both Biologics and Small Molecules play a crucial role in managing musculoskeletal disorders by alleviating symptoms, reducing inflammation, or promoting tissue repair.</p></p>
