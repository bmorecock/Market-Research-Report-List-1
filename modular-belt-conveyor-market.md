<p><p>The modular belt conveyor market consists of two main types: open belt conveyor and enclosed belt conveyor. </p><p>Open belt conveyor refers to a system with a belt that is exposed, allowing for easier access and visibility of products being transported. It is often used in applications where items are larger or irregularly shaped. </p><p>On the other hand, enclosed belt conveyor features a protective casing around the belt, preventing spillage and protecting the product from external elements such as dust or moisture. This type is commonly utilized in industries with strict hygiene requirements or for delicate items that need extra protection.</p></p>
