<p><p>DVT (Deep Vein Thrombosis) pumps, also known as compression pumps, are medical devices used to prevent blood clots by promoting blood circulation in the lower extremities. These pumps provide intermittent pressure to the leg, mimicking the natural muscle contractions that help prevent blood pooling and clot formation.</p><p>The DVT pumps market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years, primarily driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of deep vein thrombosis, the growing geriatric population, and the rising number of surgical procedures. Additionally, technological advancements in DVT pumps have improved their efficacy and patient comfort, further propelling market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, increasing awareness regarding the benefits of DVT pumps in preventing blood clots during long-haul flights and extended periods of immobility has also contributed to market expansion.
