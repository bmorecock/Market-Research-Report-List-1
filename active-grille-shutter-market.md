<p><p>An active grille shutter refers to a system installed in the front grille of a vehicle to regulate the flow of air through the radiator and engine compartment. It consists of adjustable shutters that open or close based on the vehicle's requirements for cooling or aerodynamics. When the engine requires cooling, the shutters open to allow air to enter, and when cooling is not necessary, the shutters close to improve aerodynamics and reduce drag. This technology has become increasingly popular in the automotive industry due to its ability to enhance fuel efficiency, reduce emissions, and improve overall vehicle performance.</p><p>The global active grille shutter market is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
