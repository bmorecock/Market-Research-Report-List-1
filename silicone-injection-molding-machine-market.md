<p><p>The Silicone Injection Molding Machine Market is categorized into two types: Horizontal Silicone Injection Molding Machine (HIMM) and Vertical Silicone Injection Molding Machine (VIMM). HIMM is a machine that operates horizontally, with the mold placed on a horizontal base. This type of machine is commonly used for high-volume production and offers advantages such as easy access to the mold and a shorter cooling time. VIMM, on the other hand, operates vertically, with the mold placed on a vertical base. VIMM is suitable for molding intricate and smaller parts due to its compact design and reduced footprint.</p></p>
