<p><strong>The Automatic Discrete Chemical Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flow Cell Based System</li><li>Direct Read System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow Cell Based System in automatic discrete chemical analyzer market refers to a system where samples are continuously pumped through a flow cell for analysis. This system allows for high throughput and continuous analysis of multiple samples. On the other hand, Direct Read System involves samples being directly loaded onto the system for analysis without the need for continuous pumping. Both systems have their own advantages and are used in various laboratory settings based on the specific requirements of the analysis.</p></p>
