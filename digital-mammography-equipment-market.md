<p><p>The digital mammography equipment market is comprised of several types of equipment. The most common types are Full Field Digital Mammography (FFDM), Digital Breast Tomosynthesis (DBT), and Others. FFDM utilizes digital receptors to capture mammographic images, providing quicker results and improved image quality compared to film-based mammography. DBT creates a 3D image of the breast by capturing multiple low-dose X-ray images from different angles, allowing better visualization of breast abnormalities. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies like Mammography CAD (Computer-Aided Detection) and Contrast-Enhanced Mammography, which aim to improve accuracy and detection in breast imaging.</p></p>
