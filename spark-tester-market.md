<p><p>The Spark Tester market can be categorized into two main types: Mains Frequency Spark Tester and High Frequency Spark Tester. A Mains Frequency Spark Tester is designed to detect faults in the insulation of electrical cables and devices operating at the frequency of the main power supply. On the other hand, a High Frequency Spark Tester is specifically designed to test high-frequency cables or devices operating at frequencies higher than the main power supply. Both types serve their respective purposes in detecting insulation faults but operate at different frequencies.</p></p>
