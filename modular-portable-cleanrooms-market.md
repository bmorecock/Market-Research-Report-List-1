<p><p>Modular portable cleanrooms come in two main types: softwall cleanrooms and hardwall cleanrooms. Softwall cleanrooms are flexible and lightweight, making them easy to assemble and modify. They are usually used for low to medium contamination control requirements. Hardwall cleanrooms are more rigid and durable, offering higher levels of contamination control and are suitable for industries with strict cleanliness standards. Both types provide controlled environments for tasks such as research, manufacturing, testing, and packaging that require a sterile or contaminant-free workspace.</p></p>
