It measures the moisture content at which a soil sample starts to shrink proportionally with further water loss. This device is crucial in geotechnical engineering as it helps assess the volume change characteristics of soils, which in turn influences construction and foundation design.</p><p>The global shrinkage limit device market is expected to witness a significant growth during the forecast period. Factors such as increasing infrastructure development, rapid urbanization, and growing construction activities are driving the demand for shrinkage limit devices.
