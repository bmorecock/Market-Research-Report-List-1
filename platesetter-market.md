<p><p>Platesetter is a device used in the printing industry to transfer digital images onto printing plates. The two main market types of Platesetter are Thermal Platesetter and UV Platesetter. Thermal Platesetter utilizes heat to expose and develop the plates, making it suitable for high-resolution printing. UV Platesetter, on the other hand, uses ultraviolet light to expose and cure the plates, enabling faster production speeds and longer plate durability. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on specific printing needs such as resolution, speed, and durability.</p></p>
