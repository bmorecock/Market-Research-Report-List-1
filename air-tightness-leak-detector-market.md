<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure Type</li><li>Direct Pressure Type</li><li>Flow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Tightness Leak Detector market comprises three main types: Differential Pressure Type, Direct Pressure Type, and Flow Type. The Differential Pressure Type detects leaks by measuring the difference in pressure between the tested area and a reference space. Direct Pressure Type detectors directly pressurize the area being tested and measure pressure changes to identify leaks. Flow Type detectors use a controlled airflow to detect any leakage. Each type offers unique advantages and can be used in various applications to ensure the air tightness of buildings or products.</p></p>
