<p><strong>The Melanoma Cancer Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) Tests</li><li>Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) Tests</li><li>Immunohistochemical (IHC) Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Melanoma Cancer Diagnostics market includes three types of tests: Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) tests, Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) tests, and Immunohistochemical (IHC) tests. FISH tests analyze genetic abnormalities by using fluorescently labeled probes. CGH tests detect changes in DNA copy numbers, helping in identifying genomic alterations. IHC tests detect specific proteins in tissues samples, aiding in the diagnosis and classification of melanoma. These tests play a crucial role in diagnosing melanoma and determining the appropriate treatment plan for patients.</p></p>
