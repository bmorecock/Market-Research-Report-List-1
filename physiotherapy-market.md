<p><p>Physiotherapy, also known as physical therapy, is a healthcare profession that focuses on evaluating, diagnosing, and treating various musculoskeletal and movement-related conditions. This therapeutic approach aims to enhance physical well-being, alleviate pain, restore mobility and functionality, and prevent further injuries through a combination of manual therapy, exercise, education, and electrotherapy.</p><p>The physiotherapy market has been experiencing significant growth due to several factors. One of the key drivers is the rising geriatric population globally, as elderly individuals are more prone to physical ailments and require rehabilitation services. Additionally, the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases and sports-related injuries has also contributed to the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology, such as the adoption of telehealth and virtual physiotherapy sessions, have further propelled the market. These innovations enable patients to receive physiotherapy services conveniently and remotely, expanding access to care and improving patient outcomes.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the demand for physiotherapy services. With many individuals experiencing post-COVID recovery complications or long-term effects of the virus, physiotherapy has become integral in their rehabilitation process.</p><p>The physiotherapy market's growth is projected to continue, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.7% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing awareness of the benefits of physiotherapy, growing healthcare infrastructure, and the integration of technology in physiotherapy practices.</p><p>Overall, the physiotherapy market is witnessing a positive trajectory, driven by the aging population, rising chronic diseases, technological advancements, and the impact of the pandemic. As individuals become more conscious of the importance of physical well-being, the demand for physiotherapy services is expected to increase, driving market growth.</p></p>
