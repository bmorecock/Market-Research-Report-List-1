<p><p>Hemorrhoid surgery instruments are specialized tools used during surgical procedures for the treatment of hemorrhoids. <p><p>The Hemorrhoid Surgery Instruments market is categorized into three types: Sclerotherapy Injectors, Infrared Coagulators, and Others. Sclerotherapy Injectors are medical devices used to inject sclerosing agents into hemorrhoidal tissues, causing them to shrink. Infrared Coagulators utilize infrared light to coagulate the blood vessels in the hemorrhoids, leading to their shrinking and elimination. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various other instruments used in hemorrhoid surgery, such as laser devices, staplers, and forceps. These instruments aid in the treatment and removal of hemorrhoids during surgical procedures.</p></p>
