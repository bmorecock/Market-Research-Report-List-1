<p><p>Platform Screen Door (PSD) is a safety system commonly used in train stations and subway platforms. The market for PSD can be categorized into three types: Full Height Closed, Half Height Open, and Full Height Open. Full Height Closed PSD completely separates the platform from the tracks with closed glass panels. Half Height Open PSD is a smaller version with panels extended up to waist level. Lastly, Full Height Open PSD has glass panels only up to a certain height, allowing passengers to fully access the tracks while maintaining safety.</p></p>
