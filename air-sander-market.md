<p><p>The Air Sander market consists of two main types: Orbital and Belt Sanders. Orbital Sanders are lightweight and commonly used for smoothing surfaces with random orbital motion. They are versatile and perfect for sanding small areas. Belt Sanders, on the other hand, are powerful tools primarily utilized for sanding larger areas quickly. They have an electric motor that spins a sanding belt, providing fast and efficient material removal. Both types of sanders offer unique features and cater to different sanding needs in various industries.</p></p>
