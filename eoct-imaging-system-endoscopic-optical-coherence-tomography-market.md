<p><p>The EOCT Imaging System, also known as Endoscopic Optical Coherence Tomography, is a medical technology used for visualizing internal organ tissues during endoscopic procedures. This system offers two market types: Balloon Optical Probe and Balloon-less Optical Probe. The Balloon Optical Probe utilizes a thin balloon that is inflated to provide a clearer view of the organ being examined. On the other hand, the Balloon-less Optical Probe does not require a balloon and is designed for a more flexible and maneuverable examination process. Both market types aim to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of endoscopic imaging.</p></p>
