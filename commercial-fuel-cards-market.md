<p><p>Commercial fuel cards are payment cards specifically designed for businesses to manage their fuel expenses. These cards come in different market types, including magnetic, optical, and chip. Magnetic fuel cards use a magnetic strip to store and transmit data, while optical fuel cards use a barcode or QR code for data storage and retrieval. Chip fuel cards, on the other hand, possess an embedded microchip that securely stores information and provides enhanced security features. These market types vary in terms of technology, data storage, and security measures to suit the needs and preferences of businesses in managing fuel expenditure.</p></p>
